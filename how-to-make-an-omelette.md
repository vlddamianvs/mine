# How to Make an Omelette

A simple guide to making a classic French omelette.

## Ingredients

- 2-3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter or oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional fillings: cheese, herbs, vegetables, ham

## Equipment

- Non-stick skillet (8-10 inch)
- Bowl
- Whisk or fork
- Spatula

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the eggs**
   - Crack eggs into a bowl
   - Add salt and pepper
   - Whisk until smooth and slightly frothy

2. **Heat the pan**
   - Place skillet over medium heat
   - Add butter or oil
   - Let it melt and coat the pan

3. **Cook the eggs**
   - Pour eggs into the hot pan
   - Let them sit for 20-30 seconds until edges begin to set
   - Use spatula to gently push cooked edges toward center
   - Tilt pan to let uncooked egg flow underneath

4. **Add fillings (optional)**
   - Sprinkle cheese, herbs, or other fillings on one half
   - Cook for another 30 seconds

5. **Fold and serve**
   - Use spatula to fold omelette in half
   - Slide onto plate
   - Serve immediately

## Tips

- Don't overcook - eggs should be tender, not rubbery
- Keep heat medium to prevent burning
- Practice the folding technique for a perfect shape
- Fresh eggs work best for flavor and texture
